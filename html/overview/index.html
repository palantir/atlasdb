Overview¶

What is AtlasDB?¶

AtlasDB is a transaction layer built on top of any generic key value store. Relational databases are good at OLTP (many small transactions) style queries and OLAP (ad-hoc large queries). AtlasDB doubles down on the OLTP style workflows at the expense of OLAP. Queries are very explicit and query time can easily be back of the enveloped.
